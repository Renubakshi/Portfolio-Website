5 EASY WAY TO GROW TALLLER IN A WEEK.

1) UJJAYI PRANAYAM

  • As the name suggests, Ujjayi means one who wins or one who is victorious.
  • Ujjayi pranayam, the head of yoga asanas, is such a breathing activity by which one can keep the body healthy.

STEPS

  • First of all, relax your body by sitting in a rug.
  • Now close your mouth and breathe through your nose with ease.
  • Keep breathing and focus your attention on your throat and feel the breath coming from the throat.
  • Continue to lie down and keep releasing until you feel that the air is reaching your chest.
  • Return to your normal state after about 4-5 minutes.

BENEFITS

  • Ujjayi pranayam strengthens our nervous system, which helps the body to grow naturally.
  • It eliminates the problem of thyroid from the root.

2) BHUJANGASANA(COBRA POSE )

  • Bhujangasana which is also indentified by the word Cobra Pose all over the world is a pose in which the structure of the body looks like a hood of a snake.

STEPS

  • First of all, lie down on your stomach in a rug.
  • Now place your palm on the ground with your shoulder straight.
  • Now place your palm on the ground with your shoulder straight.
  • After 5 seconds come back to your old state.
  • Do this action 5-10 times daily, do it only 2-4 times in the initial days.

benefits

  • It generates stretch in the body, which makes the body shapely and beautiful.
  • Bhujangasana is also helpful in widening the boy's chest.

3) NATRAJASANA

  • The origin of the word “Natrajasana” is from Nataraja, one of the thousands of names of the Lord Shiva.
  • Natrajasana is a type of dance pose called the Father of all dance poses.

STEPS

  • First of all, stand in a posture of Attention on one posture.
  • Now bend the right leg and try to hold your ankle with the right hand from behind the body.
  • Do not bend the right hip at all and try to stay in this position for as long as possible.
  • Repeat the same action with your left leg, then come back to normal position.

BENEFITS

  • Natarajasana strengthens the ankle, hip and chest.
  • Natarajasana maintains physical balance which leads to growth in the body.

4) PASCHIMOTTANASANA

  • Paschimottanasana, mainly a major part of Hatha yoga.
  • Paschimottanasana is also known as Seated Forward Bend or Intense Dorsal Stretch or BackStretching Pose.

STEPS

  • Extend your legs and sit on the rug.
  • Make sure that your backbone is straight.
  • Now inhale the breath and tighten the ankles with your hands, your hands should remain straight.
  • Place your hands in such a posture that a 45-degree angle is formed between your hands and feet.
  • Now slowly exhale and bring your to the normal position.
  • Repeat this step 10-15 times daily.

BENEFITS

  • It strengthens your digestive system.
  • By doing this asana daily, your mind will be free from fatigue as well as you will feel relaxed.
